Overview

This project presents an immersive audio framework that combines Ultra-Wideband (UWB) technology with real-time 3D spatial audio rendering. By tracking user’s spatial position with UWB tags and anchors, the system dynamically adjusts object-based audio rendering in Unity 3D using Steam Audio. As users move through a physical space, their position influences how they hear sound objects.
