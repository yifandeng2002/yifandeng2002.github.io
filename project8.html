Overview

Unlike typical soundscape apps that rely on toggling multiple switches or adjusting complex sliders, we designed an approach that adopts a more intuitive and user-friendly interaction strategy. At the core of the playback interface is a 2D matrix: the X-axis corresponds to different sound frequencies, allowing users to make nuanced choices between “soft” and “crisp” tones, while the Y-axis lets users adjust between “simple” and “complex” textures to fine-tune the overall richness of the soundscape.

Features

The texture of the sound can be adjusted along the X-axis, allowing users to control the degree of softness or clarity. This is achieved by applying a filter that alters the balance of high and low frequency content. Along the Y-axis, the complexity of the soundscape is modified by changing the number of sound layers mixed together.
