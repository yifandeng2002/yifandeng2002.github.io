Industry Relevance & Future Directions

The research being conducted at ITP directly addresses several key trends in the music technology industry:

Spatial Audio Revolution: With the rise of AR/VR and immersive experiences, spatial audio interfaces are becoming crucial for next-generation entertainment platforms.
Accessibility in Tech: There's a growing recognition that inclusive design isn't just ethical—it's innovative, often leading to solutions that benefit all users.
AI Integration: The integration of AI in creative tools is transforming how artists work, requiring new interaction paradigms and ethical considerations.

These research directions align closely with HARMAN's mission to develop connected technologies that enhance human experiences, particularly in automotive and professional audio environments.
