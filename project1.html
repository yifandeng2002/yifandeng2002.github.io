Overview

The Chimes Widget is a Max.for.live device designed to bring simple fun and inspiration to music creators by generating natural chime sounds based on wind speed. Built with MaxMSP and integrated into Ableton Live, it uses a set of 15 unique chime samples that are triggered in randomized patterns influenced by either manual control or real-time wind data from the US Weather Service.
