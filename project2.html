Overview

Indoor Chimes is an acoustic sound device that plays chime sounds based on real-world wind data and tactile user input. The system blends natural influence with user control: wind speed from a weather API determines the playback intensity and intervals of the chimes, while physical knobs allow users to adjust overall volume, base rhythm intervals, and the degree of randomness in playback.
